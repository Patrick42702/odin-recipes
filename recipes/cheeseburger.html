Cheeseburger

Description

This is one of my favorite foods, a delicious cheeseburger! A classic American food that is enjoyed all around America!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • American cheese slice
  • Beef patty
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  1. 1. Put the burger on the grill
  2. 2. Cook until well done
  3. 3. Take the burger once cooked off the grill and place onto the bun
  4. 4. Add cheese, tomato, onion, spices
  5. 5. Enjoy!
