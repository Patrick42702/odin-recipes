Spaghetti

Description

An Italian staple, spaghetti is a tasty pasta that is easy to make yet delicious!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add pasta to water.
  3. Boil pasta for 5-7 minutes until desired texture reached.
  4. Once complete, drain pasta.
  5. Place pasta back into the pot.
  6. Add tomato sauce and mix.
  7. Enjoy!
