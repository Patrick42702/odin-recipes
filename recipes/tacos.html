Tacos

Description

An excellent Mexican dish, tacos are easy to make and still super tasty!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef or Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook your protein of choice.
  2. Prepare all the side ingredients.
  3. Place the ingredients you want on the tortilla.
  4. Don't worry about utensils, eat with your hands! Enjoy!
